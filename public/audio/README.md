# Audio Files

Place your audio preview files here.

## Required Files:
- `preview.mp3` - A sample 30-second audio preview that will play when users click the play button

## Supported Formats:
- MP3 (recommended)
- WAV
- OGG

## Instructions:
1. Add your audio file named `preview.mp3` to this directory
2. The audio will automatically play when users click the play button on the Audio Stories page

## Temporary Solution:
If you don't have an audio file yet, you can:
1. Download any royalty-free music/audio sample
2. Rename it to `preview.mp3`
3. Place it in this folder

The audio player is now fully functional and will play sound once you add the audio file!
